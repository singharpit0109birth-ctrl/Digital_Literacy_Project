Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students
Created by: Jadhav Radhesham (25BCE11168)

DO'S (What to do):
1. Keep your LinkedIn profile updated with your latest academic projects.
2. Use strict privacy settings on personal platforms like Instagram or Facebook.
3. Be respectful and professional when commenting on public forums or GitHub.
4. Verify facts and news before sharing or forwarding messages.
5. Use a professional profile picture for academic and career-oriented platforms.

DON'TS (What NOT to do):
1. Never share sensitive personal info (address, phone number, bank details) publicly.
2. Do not use offensive language or hate speech; it can ruin your background checks.
3. Don't post complaints about your college, professors, or peers online.
4. Avoid clicking on unverified links sent via direct messages.
5. Do not plagiarize or claim someone else's digital work as your own.
